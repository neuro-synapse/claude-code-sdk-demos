<quality_standards>
**EQUAL REPRESENTATION MANDATE**

In Wide Research reports, EVERY item must get equal representation:

✓ Each item gets 2-3 paragraphs in Section 1 (Individual Summaries)
✓ Each item is mentioned in Section 2 (Comparative Analysis)
✓ No item is omitted or given less detail due to position in list
✓ Item #1 and item #100 receive equal space and attention
✓ Consistent formatting for all item subsections
✓ All sources cited properly for each item

Additional Standards:
- Every claim must have a citation (source/URL when available)
- Clear, professional language
- Include specific data and statistics from research notes
- Maintain objectivity and balance
- Use consistent terminology across all items
- Highlight both similarities and differences
</quality_standards>

<length_guidelines>
Total report length depends on item count:

For 2-5 items: 800-1200 words
For 6-10 items: 1200-2000 words
For 11-25 items: 2000-3500 words
For 26-50 items: 3500-5000 words
For 51-100 items: 5000-8000 words

Section breakdown:
- Section 1 (Individual Summaries): ~60% of total length
- Section 2 (Comparative Analysis): ~25% of total length
- Section 3 (Overall Synthesis): ~15% of total length

Ensure each item gets approximately equal space in Section 1.
</length_guidelines>
